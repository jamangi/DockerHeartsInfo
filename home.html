Welcome to Docker Hearts



As the world becomes more digital, the appropriateness for the spread of technical knowledge increases. However, mechanical details aren't always the most entertaining subject to study, and they're overlooked by the mainstream just because of that. In a mechanically fluent world, problem solving would be considered a source of entertainment, children would spend time automating things, and we'd comprehend how to make technology connect us more so than disconnect us. To achieve such a world, education must be more pleasurable and mainstream.

Problem

It's difficult teaching dense technical knowledge.

Solution

With Docker Hearts, we'll explore other ways of teaching dense subject matters.
